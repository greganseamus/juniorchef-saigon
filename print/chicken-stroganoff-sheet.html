Chicken Strog-anoff

for 4!
Chef's secret
Add the sour cream off the heat and stir gently — boiling it will make it split and go grainy. Patience is the secret to a glossy sauce.

Ingredients For 4

  • 600g chicken breast (cut into strips)
  • 250g mushrooms (sliced)
  • 1 large onion (thinly sliced)
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tbsp sweet paprika
  • 250ml chicken stock
  • 200ml sour cream
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt and pepper

To serve

  • 300g egg noodles or pasta
  • Fresh parsley (chopped)

Directions

1
Brown the Chicken

High heat with oil, 2 min each side — golden on the outside but not cooked through yet. Remove and set aside. This step builds flavour.

2
Soften the Onion

Same pan, add butter on medium heat. Cook the onion 6–8 min until soft and golden. Low and slow is the key here.

3
Add Mushrooms and Garlic

Stir in sliced mushrooms and garlic. Cook 4–5 min until the mushrooms go soft and juicy and the garlic smells amazing.

4
Dust with Paprika

Sprinkle the paprika over everything and stir for 30 seconds — it'll go toasty and smell incredible. Don't let it burn.

5
Build the Sauce

Pour in the chicken stock, return the browned chicken and simmer for 8 min until the sauce thickens and chicken is cooked through.

6
The Sour Cream Moment

Take the pan OFF the heat! Stir in sour cream slowly and gently until the sauce turns glossy and smooth. No boiling allowed.

7
Cook the Noodles and Serve

Cook noodles al dente, pile into bowls, spoon over the stroganoff and scatter fresh chopped parsley on top.
