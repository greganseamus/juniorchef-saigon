Summer Berry
Shortcake

for 4!
Chef's secret
Cold ingredients are the secret to flaky shortcake — freeze your butter before you start!

Ingredients For 4

  • 200g plain flour
  • 80g granulated sugar
  • 10g baking powder
  • Pinch of baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 90g cold unsalted butter
  • 90g cold plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 egg (for egg wash)
  • 3 tbsp cold water
  • 200g mixed berries
  • 200ml heavy cream
  • Extra sugar for berries

Directions

1
Chill Everything First

Preheat oven to 200°C. Pop the flour, butter, and yogurt in the freezer for 10 minutes. Cold ingredients are the secret to flaky shortcake.

2
Prep the Berries

Slice the strawberries and mix with the rest of the berries. Sprinkle with a little sugar and set aside — they'll go juicy while you work.

3
Make the Dough

Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Add cold butter in small pieces and rub in with your fingertips until the mixture looks like coarse, lumpy sand.

4
Add the Wet Ingredients

Whisk yogurt, egg, and cold water together. Pour in and stir until it just barely comes together — lumpy is fine, do NOT overwork the dough.

5
Cut the Shortcakes

Flour your surface and pat dough into a thick square — about 2.5cm high. Cut into rounds and bake 12–15 minutes until puffed and golden.

6
Whip the Cream

While the shortcakes bake, whip the cream to soft peaks — it should hold its shape but still look fluffy and light.

7
Assemble and Eat

Split a warm shortcake open. Pile on the juicy berries, spoon over the cream, and eat straight away. Summer in a mouthful.
