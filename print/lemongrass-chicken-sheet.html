Lemongrass
Chicken
Salad

for 4!
Chef's secret
Bruise the lemongrass stalk with the back of your knife — it releases ten times more flavour than slicing alone.

Ingredients For 4

  • 500g chicken thighs (boneless)
  • 2 stalks lemongrass (bruised)
  • 3 tbsp fish sauce
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 small red chili (sliced)
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 200g dried rice vermicelli
  • 1 cucumber
  • Large handful fresh mint
  • Large handful Vietnamese coriander (rau ram)
  • 1 carrot (julienned)
  • 60g roasted peanuts (crushed)

Nuoc Cham Dressing

  • 3 tbsp fish sauce
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 clove garlic (minced)
  • 1 small chili (finely sliced)
  • 4 tbsp water

Directions

1
The Magic Marinade

Mix fish sauce, lime juice, sugar, chili, and garlic. Add the bruised lemongrass stalks and toss in the chicken. Marinate for 20 minutes.

2
Soak the Noodles

Cover the rice vermicelli in boiling water for 5 minutes. Drain and rinse well under cold water to stop them sticking.

3
Make the Nuoc Cham

Shake all the dressing ingredients together in a jar and taste — add more lime for tart, more sugar for sweet.

4
Cook the Chicken

High heat, 4–5 minutes each side until golden and cooked through. Slice the thickest piece to check — no pink allowed!

5
Slice and Shred

Rest the chicken for 5 minutes, then slice thinly across the grain for maximum tenderness.

6
Build the Bowl

Noodles first, then cucumber, carrot, and herbs — tumble it all together so the colours mix beautifully.

7
Finish and Dress

Top with chicken, scatter peanuts, drizzle nuoc cham — don't drown it. Wrap and eat Vietnamese style!
