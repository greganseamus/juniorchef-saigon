Lentil Bolog-nese

for 4!
Chef's secret
The soffritto is the foundation — don't rush the onion, carrot, and celery. Low heat, 10 minutes. That's where all the sweetness comes from.

Ingredients For 4

  • 200g red or green lentils (rinsed)
  • 2 onions (finely diced)
  • 2 carrots (finely diced)
  • 2 stalks celery (finely diced)
  • 4 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 400g tin crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 250ml vegetable stock
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

To serve

  • 350g spaghetti or rigatoni
  • Parmesan or nutritional yeast (to finish)

Directions

1
Build the Soffritto

Low heat, olive oil, onion, carrot and celery — 10 min, stirring often, until soft and sweet. Don't rush this. It's the whole foundation.

2
Add Garlic and Paste

Push the veg to the side, add garlic and tomato paste, cook 2 min until fragrant and the paste darkens slightly in colour.

3
Add Lentils

Stir in the rinsed lentils and mix everything together so they're coated in all that flavour.

4
Pour In the Sauce

Add tinned tomatoes, stock, oregano and basil. Stir and bring everything to a gentle simmer.

5
Simmer and Thicken

25–30 min on low heat until the lentils are soft and the sauce is thick and glossy. Stir every 5 min so it doesn't catch.

6
Taste and Adjust

Salt, pepper, more oregano? Trust your tastebuds. Season until it tastes exactly right to you.

7
Cook the Pasta and Serve

Boil pasta al dente, reserve a cup of pasta water, toss together adding pasta water to loosen — finish with parmesan.
