Korean Beef
Bulgogi Skewers

Korea's most famous "fire meat" — beef marinated in soy, sugar, sesame, and grated pear, folded onto skewers and caramelised on a hot pan. Kids learn enzyme tenderising, skewer technique, and how to build a Korean-style wrap with lettuce and rice.

Chef's Secret
Enzymes in the grated pear actually break down the meat and make it melt-in-your-mouth tender. Don't skip the pear!
