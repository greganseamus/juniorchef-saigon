Summer Berry
Shortcakes

A classic British-American summer treat — flaky shortcake, juicy macerated berries, and whipped cream. The secret is keeping everything cold. Kids master baking science and learn why butter temperature matters.

Chef's Secret
Cold ingredients are the secret to flaky shortcake — freeze your butter before you start!
