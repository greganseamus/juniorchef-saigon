Russian · Comfort 40 min · Ages 6–12 · For 4

Chicken
Stroganoff

A Russian classic — golden chicken, soft mushrooms, and a glossy sour cream sauce over egg noodles. Kids learn the art of the sear, how to build a pan sauce, and the one critical rule for sour cream: add it off the heat.

Chef's Secret
Add the sour cream off the heat and stir gently — boiling it will make it split and go grainy. Patience is the secret to a glossy sauce.
