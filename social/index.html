Usage Notes

Exporting

Open any template full-size, set browser zoom to 200%, then screenshot. This gives you 2× pixel density (effectively 2160px wide for the square posts).

Captions

Suggested captions live in comments inside each template file. Copy, adjust the recipe name, and paste into your scheduler.

New Recipes

Swap the hero photo and recipe name — all other styles inherit from the design tokens. Takes 2 minutes per recipe.
