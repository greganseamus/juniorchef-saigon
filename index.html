World Recipes

JuniorChef Saigon runs cooking clubs inside international schools across Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. Kids ages 6–12 follow a real recipe from a different part of the world each session — no veg pre-cut, no shortcuts. They cook, taste, adjust, and take home a recipe sheet so they can make it again on the weekend.

Our free recipe sheets and worksheets are designed for the classroom and the home kitchen. Download, print, and cook.
